Welcome To Barisal Polytechnic Institute

Barisal Polytechnic Institute is the largest Polytechnic Institute in the country and the highest seat of learning in the South region of Bangladesh. The Polytechnic has crossed 48 years of its foundation opening its door for the students with a solemn promise to spread quality of Technical education and research on 1962. Situated at the main campus of the Institute is very close to Barisal city. The Barisal Polytechnic Institute is basically an educational and Practical Experiment Orintation institution.
Provide instruction in engineering, technology and technical and vocational education and in such branches of learning connected with the above fields as per requirement of the Member.
